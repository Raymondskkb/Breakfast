Today's recipe will be ... Harsh Browns

cooking Time - 15 minutes

The perfect breakfast for you and your Family

This originated in New york in 1890, Harsh browns are a common breakfast snack in modern day restuarants

Ingredients Needed
  • Peelled and Washed potatoes
  • Eggs
  • Milk (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper(optional)
  • Cooking oil
Steps for Preparation
  1. Grate the raw potatoes in a clean bowl
    • Transfer the potatoes on a clean tea towel or kitchen towel ( Reason: gently pat dry and remove starch
    • Cover the potatoes with a towel and press both hands to make sure all excess liquid starch removed
  2. Crack eggs in a bowl
  3. Add milk
  4. Add salt to taste
  5. Season with black pepperoptional
  6. Beat the Eggs/ Whisk
  7. Transfer the pat dried grated potatoes to the beaten eggs
  8. Use a fork or whisk to gently combine well.
    • make sure salt is enough and tasty
    • you can add a few spices of your choice
  9. Heat cooking oil into a pan. Oil should be very hot
  10. Use spoon to scoop out the potatoe mixture from the mixing bowl

    11. (Cooking starts)

  11. Use a slicer / egg turner
  12. Watch till it's ready
